Yumi Yuusha (Male... Yumi means 'bow' (military) ) 
Yumi's personality is based around scifi and fighting. his outfit is based on a Medievil night (Helmet, Armour), Robocop (Robotic limbs, Cobra Assault Canon) and the Predator (shoulder cannon, Arm computer)

Yume Yuusha (Female... Yume means 'dream')
Yume's personality is based on Fantasy and the Occult... Her outfit is based on Blade (Trousers weapons, boots) & Hina Ichigo From Rozen maiden(Inverted black pink dress, hair bow)... it's not really clear in the game graphics, but she has a clove of garlic around her neck, and vials of holy water attached to the sleves and trim of her dress.
